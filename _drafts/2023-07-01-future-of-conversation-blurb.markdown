I imagine a world where people can converse with each other in a language they feel most comfortable in. Each person speaks their language, and another can make sense of it. What technologies will it require? Let's see.

In this world, AA and BB speak languages X and Y from around the globe. They happen to run into each other. AA starts talking in X. BB's ears perceive what AA is speaking. Signals from BB's ears are routed to an extension of BB's body called LFree. Two years back, BB implanted LFree. It initially caused BB much headache, but BB's brain eventually learned to make sense of LFree's alien signals with some help from LFree's neuro-biotronic adapter. LFree is like another body organ powered by ADP molecules. LFree is pre-programmed to recognize most languages spoken by humanity. Its soft wiring converts languages to a standard language-agnostic embedding—BB's brain has integrated the signals from LFree into its representation. The process was similar to learning to read leaps as far as BB's brain was concerned. 

Since AA also has an LFree implant, AA and BB communicate with each other within the comforts of their mother tongues, X and Y!
