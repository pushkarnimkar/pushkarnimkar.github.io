Women are kept out of the labor force by care responsibilities at a rate 18× higher than men. 45% of working-age women cite care as their reason for not working vs. just 5% of men.
Source: ILO, October 2024
Daily hours spent on unpaid care work
Global average hours per day · UNDP / ILO
Women (4h 25m) Men (1h 23m)
Women perform 76.2% of all unpaid care globally — a workload equivalent to $11 trillion per year if compensated (9% of global GDP). Each extra hour of care shrinks a woman's chance of paid work by 38%.
Source: UNDP · UN Women 2025

For every 100 men promoted to manager, only 93 women are — and only 74 women of color. The "broken rung" at first promotion is the single biggest obstacle women face on the path to senior leadership.
Source: McKinsey & LeanIn.Org, Women in the Workplace 2025
