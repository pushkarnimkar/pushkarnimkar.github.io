I'm Pushkar Nimkar, a passionate computer engineer who ventured into computational economics. After earning my degree from the Pune Institute of Computer Technology, I joined Intangles Labs as the first non-founding data science team member. My experience there was diverse, ranging from leading the development of a flagship fuel tracking algorithm, creating predictive models for various vehicle components, to automotive safety applications using computer vision.

Undeterred by the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, I used this time to explore economics and game theory, eventually pursuing a Masters in Economics and Computation at Duke University. There, I deepened my understanding of economics, algorithms, and advanced machine learning techniques.

Now, I'm excitedly seeking new opportunities that intersect my unique blend of skills in computer science and economics. In my downtime, I find joy in the melodies of good music, the aroma of a well-brewed cup of coffee, and the strategic game of bullet chess.
