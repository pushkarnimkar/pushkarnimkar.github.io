# AGI is achievable!

I believe human intelligence is an outcomes millions of years of evolutionary pressure. This gave rise to complex biological optimizations at hardware level as well as at algorithmic level in a steady state. I look at this with awe and have great respect for evolution. But I do think that the human intelligence, at least at the outcome level, is replicable. As of 2026, we might be decades away from it.

(Comment: this is a bit vague, should define what does replication at outcome-level mean?)

# Paths to prosperity matter

To an individual, nothing could be worse than knowing marginal increase in effort will result in a marginal increase in outcome. 

World is full of bad incentive structures and outright coercion. I feel strongly for those who do not have the above as given.
